Server Rules

Please follow these rules to maintain a fun environment for everyone.

01

No Hacking

Use of modified clients (x-ray, reach, fly) or unfair advantages is strictly prohibited and results in a permanent ban.

02

Respect Players

No racism, sexism, homophobia, harassment, or extreme toxicity. Be respectful to all members.

03

No Spamming

Avoid flooding the chat, excessive caps, character spam, or advertising other servers.

04

No Griefing

Destroying or altering other players' builds without permission is not allowed outside of designated PvP/raid zones.

05

Listen to Staff

The staff's word is final. Do not argue with staff members in public chat; use discord tickets for disputes.
